What is Lifely?

Lifely connects you with food directly from the producers, sidestepping the supermarkets. We make exceptional food and deliver it directly to your door in plastic free packaging.

Lifely Eat

Lifely Eat is our range of healthy, simple and incredibly fresh handmade ready meals and convenience food. Every ingredient is sourced using our community of hand selected producers, all of whom demonstrate respect for the environment, humane treatment of animals, sustainable and natural practices.

Join the Lifely tribe

Our Farms

We are 100% committed to sustainable regenerative farming processes and educating the next generation through our FARM SCHOOL.

Behind the brand

Our food choices are deeply connected to climate change. Most of our food is produced using methods that reduce biodiversity, decimate soil and climate change.

We believe our food can and should be part of the solution to the environmental crisis - grown, harvested and produced in ways that restore our land, water and wildlife.
